Assumptions & Disclaimer

This tool provides general financial modelling only and does not constitute financial advice. Contribution rates are modelled at 3.5% employee and 3.5% employer for salary income (7% total), and 3.5% for self-employed income. Actual KiwiSaver contribution rates are subject to Inland Revenue rules. Returns assumed: 7% (growth assets), 3.5% (income assets), inflation 2.5%, fees 1%. Results are estimates only and actual outcomes will vary.
